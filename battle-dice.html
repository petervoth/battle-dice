📋 Rules

Dice move exactly their top-face value in squares.
Travel in a straight line or one L-shape turn (90°).
Cannot jump over pieces. Capture only on the final square.

When two routes reach the same square, ▲ gold = rows-first, ▲ teal = cols-first. Click a triangle to choose.

King — moves one square in any direction.

Capture the enemy King to win!

Show Outcomes — toggles the final dice value overlay on valid move squares.

Inspect Die — click any die on the board to load it into the 3D viewer. Drag to rotate.
