🏆 How Scores Work

Scores are only awarded for human wins vs AI. Losses score zero.
Games with Special Mechanics applied are excluded.

Formula
Difficulty × (Efficiency + Speed) × Outcomes
Difficulty — multiplies your entire score:
Easy ×1.0 · Medium ×1.5 · Hard ×2.5 · Expert ×4.0
Efficiency — rewards fewer rounds.
Shorter games score higher, with a guaranteed minimum of 50.
Speed — rewards thinking quickly (your time only).
Faster decisions score higher, with a guaranteed minimum of 50.
Outcomes Penalty — if Show Outcomes was ever enabled:
Clean game ×1.0  ·  Outcomes used ×0.75

📋 Rules

Dice move exactly their top-face value in squares.
Travel in a straight line or one L-shape turn (90°).
Cannot jump over pieces. Capture only on the final square.

When two routes reach the same square, ▲ gold = rows-first, ▲ teal = cols-first. Click a triangle to choose.

King — moves one square in any direction.

Capture the enemy King to win!

Show Outcomes — toggles the final dice value overlay on valid move squares.

Inspect Die — click any die on the board to load it into the 3D viewer. Drag to rotate.
